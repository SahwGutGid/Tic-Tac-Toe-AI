How to Play

  • Click on any empty cell to make your move
  • The AI will automatically respond based on the selected difficulty
  • First player to get 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • On Hard difficulty, the AI uses the Minimax algorithm and cannot be beaten
